Weekly screen time calculator and evaluator

This program asks users how much time they spend on screens everyone day and with these inputs it calculates their weekly screen time. It also gives feedback that can be either good or bad, depending on the amount of hours, and if someone enters an invalid input, such as words, the program also returns a message that asks for a valid number.

Example output based on input and weekly screen time calculated:

inputs:
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Sunday?1
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Monday?2
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Tuesday?4
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Wednesday?3
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Thursday?6
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Friday?2
How many hours did you spend on your phone on Saturday?1

feedback:
Your screen time this week was 19.0 hours, which exceeds recommended weekly screen time.
